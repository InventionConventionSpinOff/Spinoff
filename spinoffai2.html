About Spin Off

Created by Sahil & Skyler | 7th Grade Inventors

Invention Convention 2026

Our Mission

We created Spin Off to bring professional-grade sports analytics to every soccer player. Using advanced sensors and AI-powered coaching, we're making data-driven training accessible to everyone.

Technology

Spin Off combines Arduino Nano 33 BLE Sense Rev 2 with Groq AI (Llama 3.3) to deliver real-time performance analytics, instant AI coaching responses, and personalized training advice.

Features

  • Real-time speed, spin, and trajectory tracking
  • AI chatbot coach (powered by Groq)
  • Shot placement visualization with field lines
  • Performance analytics and trends
  • Session export and sharing
  • Instant technique tips and advice
