About Spin Off

Created by Sahil & Skyler | 7th Grade Inventors

Invention Convention 2026

Our Mission

We created Spin Off to bring professional-grade sports analytics to every soccer player. Using advanced sensors and free AI coaching, we're making data-driven training accessible to everyone.

Technology

Spin Off combines Arduino Nano 33 BLE Sense Rev 2 with free AI (Hugging Face) to deliver real-time performance analytics, personalized coaching, and custom training plans tailored to your skill level.

Features

  • Real-time speed, spin, and trajectory tracking
  • Free AI-powered coaching feedback (no API key needed!)
  • Personalized training plan generation
  • Shot placement visualization with soccer field lines
  • Performance analytics and trends
  • Session export and sharing
